Our History

Prior to World War I, J.N. "Polly" Parrott was working in Dallas for The Texas Company. After serving in World War I, he returned and became a consignee for most parts of Texas west of Dallas. In 1923, he married Ruth Alderman, settled in Dallas, and formed Consumers Gasoline Company, initially peddling gasolines and oils from his own truck.

In 1951, his son G.N. "Polly" Parrott married Jo Medford and joined the firm. Later, sons Scott and Randy were born. J.N. and G.N. formed new companies: Oils, Inc. and Parrott Oil Corporation. In the early 1970s, these companies merged into Parrott Oil Corp. The company experienced losses when J.N. passed in 1989, Ruth in 1990, and Scott in 1991.

Ranparr, Inc. was originally formed as an automotive equipment company in 1988. When Parrott Oil Corp. sold its assets in 1993, Ranparr, Inc. began selling motor fuels under the direction of Randy Parrott as President. His children Jaime and Justin now work with the company to continue family traditions into the future. Today, Ranparr, Inc. serves over 40 cities in Texas.

Benefits of On-Site Fuel Systems:

  1. Inventory Control: All purchased fuel is used in your business, vehicles, and equipment with complete visibility.
  2. Labor Savings: Keep employees on-site and productive. No more trips to fuel up at service stations.
  3. Simplified Accounting: One invoice per delivery reconciled to a monthly statement eliminates ticket management.
  4. Increased Production: Save an average of 15 minutes per driver per day—that's 60 hours of production annually per driver.
  5. Consistent Quality: Achieve optimal fleet performance with controlled, uniform fuel quality.
  6. Reduced Cost: Annual savings of several thousand dollars when accounting for all operational benefits plus our competitive pricing.

Let Ranparr, Inc. design a TCEQ-compliant fuel system to meet your individual requirements. All plans are permitted before installation and meet or exceed local fire department codes.

Equipment Division

Ranparr, Inc. is pleased to have introduced the vaulted aboveground protected tank to the Dallas-Fort Worth market in 1993. In fact, we placed the first protected tanks in the State of Texas! Our first tanks were manufactured in California in accordance with the strict standards of the California Air Resources Board (C.A.R.B.). Today, our tanks are made right here in the DFW metroplex—right here in Texas!

Our Expertise:

Since 1993, Ranparr, Inc. has installed hundreds of aboveground protected tanks. Our dedicated staff is ready to meet your demands with comprehensive services:
